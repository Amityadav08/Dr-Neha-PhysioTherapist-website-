About Dr. Neha Goraksha Bidvai

Dr. Neha Goraksha Bidvai is a passionate and innovative physiotherapist with over 12 years of dedicated experience in restoring mobility and alleviating pain. Her patient-centric approach is built on evidence-based care, empowering individuals to reclaim a vibrant, active life. A proud graduate of Sancheti Institute College of Physiotherapy, Pune, and a top-ranked Master’s degree holder in Physiotherapy (Musculoskeletal Sciences) from Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Hospital, Dr. Neha’s academic excellence forms the backbone of her clinical expertise. Her impressive career includes key roles at renowned institutions like Kokilaben Hospital, Andheri, and over 8 years as a successful private practitioner.

Certified in advanced techniques such as:

  • Mulligan Concept
  • Dry Needling
  • Kinesiology Taping
  • Pilates
  • Neurodynamics

Passion for Patient Care: Beyond merely treating symptoms, Dr. Neha is devoted to uncovering the root cause of pain and dysfunction. Her personalized treatment plans not only relieve discomfort but also equip patients with the tools to take charge of their own health, ensuring sustainable, long-term recovery.

If you’re seeking a trusted physiotherapist who prioritizes your well-being and recovery, Dr. Neha Goraksha Bidvai is here to guide you on your journey to a pain-free, active life.

Our Speciality

Discover our comprehensive, specialized physiotherapy treatments designed for optimal recovery.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Focus: Treatment of musculoskeletal conditions affecting bones, joints, muscles, and tendons.

Conditions Treated: Arthritis, Joint Pain, Neck Pain, Lower Back Pain, Spondylitis, Slipped Disc, Frozen Shoulder, Plantar Fasciitis, Fibromyalgia.

Objective: Alleviate pain, improve mobility, and restore functional movement.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Focus: Specialized care for older adults with comprehensive evaluation of musculoskeletal and balance issues.

Conditions Treated: Balance Impairment, Joint Pain, Generalized Weakness, Postural Instability, and related concerns.

Objective: Enhance mobility, reduce fall risk, and promote independence in daily activities.

Post Surgery Physiotherapy

Focus: Rehabilitation after surgical procedures.

Procedures Supported: Knee & Hip Replacement, Ligament Reconstruction, Rotator Cuff Repair, Fracture Fixation, Spine Surgeries.

Objective: Facilitate healing, improve strength, and regain range of motion.

Oncology Physiotherapy

Focus: Supportive care during cancer treatment.

Programs Offered: MOBILITY & STEEL (Pre & Post-operative), Lymphatic Drainage, Fatigue Management, Resistance Training.

Objective: Enhance mobility, reduce treatment side effects, and improve overall well-being.

Sports Injury Physiotherapy

Focus: Rehabilitation for sports-related injuries.

Conditions Treated: Ligament Injuries, Ankle Sprain, Shoulder Injuries, Meniscal Injuries, Overuse Injuries, Tennis Elbow.

Objective: Restore athletic performance and prevent future injuries.
