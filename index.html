About Dr. Neha Goraksha Bidvai

Dr. Neha Goraksha Bidvai is a passionate and innovative physiotherapist with 15 years of experience in restoring mobility and alleviating pain. Her patient-centric approach is rooted in evidence-based care, empowering individuals to lead active, pain-free lives.

A proud graduate of Sancheti Institute College of Physiotherapy, Pune, and a top-ranked Master’s degree holder in Physiotherapy (Musculoskeletal Sciences) from Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Hospital, Dr. Neha’s academic excellence forms the foundation of her clinical expertise. Her career spans key roles at renowned institutions like Kokilaben Hospital, Andheri, along with over eight years as a successful private practitioner.

Commitment to Patient Care

Dr. Neha goes beyond treating symptoms—she is dedicated to identifying the root cause of pain and dysfunction. Her personalized treatment plans not only provide relief but also empower patients with the knowledge and strategies needed for long-term recovery.

If you’re looking for a trusted physiotherapist who prioritizes your well-being, Dr. Neha Goraksha Bidvai is here to guide you on your journey to better health.
